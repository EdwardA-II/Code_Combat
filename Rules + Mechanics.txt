*********************************** RULES AND MECHANICS ***********************************
1. This is a turn-based game where you get to go first!

2. There are 10 CHARACTERS, 5 CLASSES (or Types) with 2 CHARACTERS each.

3. Each Class of Character has their own strengths and weaknesses.

4. You can pick one Character and choose from a variety of items that have various effects.

5. All Characters have 1000 HEALTH.

6. YOU CAN ATTACK or use and ITEM during your move.
