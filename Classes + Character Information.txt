***************************** CLASSES + CHARACTER INFORMATION *****************************

** TANKS **
High DEFENSE, Low ACCURACY, Modertate ATTACK, Moderate ITEMS

- ATLAS
  Ability: Endure
  - ATLAS increases their DEFENSE (+100 PTS) for the next 2 moves.

- RONNIE COLEMAN
  Ability: Lightweight Baby!
  - RONNIE COLEMAN gains bonus DAMAGE (+50 PTS) for the next 2 moves.


** MARKSMAN **
Low DEFENSE, High ACCURACY, Moderate ATTACK, Limited ITEMS (1 ITEM)

- DEADSHOT
  Ability: Cut and Run
  - DEADSHOT gets a temporary DAMAGE buff of 1.5x normal attack DAMAGE for 1 turn.

- HAWKEYE
  Ability: Ronin
  - RONIN bypasses their OPPONENTs DEFENSE and deals 150 DAMAGE directly to their health.


** THIEF **
Low DEFENSE, Moderate ATTACK, Moderate ACCURACY, High ITEMS (5 ITEMS)

- KAZUMA
  Ability: STEAL
  - KAZUMA takes one item from the OPPONENTs inventory permanently.

- REVY
  Ability: Two Hands
  - REVY deals 50 DAMAGE and takes one item from the OPPONENTs inventory for 1 move.


** SOLDIER **
Moderate DEFENSE, ATTACK, ACCURACY, ITEMS

- PATHFINDER
  Ability: The Way
  - PATHFINDER evades their OPPONENTs attack and becomes invincible for 1 turn.


- CHAMPION
  Ability: Victory
  - CHAMPION raises their DEFENSE and ATTACK by 50 PTS each.


** MAGE ** 
Low DEFENSE, High ATTACK, Moderate ACCURACY, No ITEMS

- RIMURU
  Ability: Gluttony
  - RIMURU does 25 DAMAGE and permanently lowers OPPONENTs DEFENSE by 100 PTS.

- MEGUMIN
  Ability: Explosion 
  - MEGUMIN casts a spell that deals 300 DAMAGE.
